HOW TO PLAY !

Game Setup

Tic-Tac-Toe is played on a 3x3 grid (9 squares).

Two players take turns:

Player 1 uses "X"

Player 2 uses "O"

Objective

The goal is to get three of your marks (X or O) in a row vertically, horizontally, or diagonally before your opponent.

How to Play

Decide who goes first (usually Player X).

Players take turns placing their mark (X or O) in an empty square.

click empty Square or Use number System by following:

7 8 9
4 5 6
3 2 1

⇒

x o x
o x o
x o x

The game continues until:

A player wins by forming a row of 3.

All 9 squares are filled, resulting in a draw (if no one wins).

If no one wins you can reset by reset button or by clicking "0".

Winning Conditions

A player wins if they get three in a row:

✅ Horizontally (e.g., top row: X X X)

✅ Vertically (e.g., first column: O O O)

✅ Diagonally (e.g., top-left to bottom-right: X X X)
